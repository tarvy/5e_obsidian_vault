# Remove Curse
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

At your touch, all curses affecting one creature or object end. If the object is a cursed magic item, its curse remains, but the spell breaks its owner's attunement to the object so it can be removed or discarded.

**Classes**: [Cleric](compendium/classes/cleric.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](compendium/classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Paladin](compendium/classes/paladin.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](compendium/classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Warlock](compendium/classes/warlock.md), [Wizard](compendium/classes/wizard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 271. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
