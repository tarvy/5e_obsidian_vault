# Magic Weapon
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.

**Classes**: [Cleric (Arcana Domain)](compendium/classes/cleric-arcana-domain-scag.md), [Cleric (War Domain)](compendium/classes/cleric-war-domain.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](compendium/classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Paladin](compendium/classes/paladin.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](compendium/classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Wizard](compendium/classes/wizard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 257. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
