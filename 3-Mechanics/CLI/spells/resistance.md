# Resistance
*cantrip, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a miniature cloak
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a `d4` and add the number rolled to one saving throw of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the saving throw. The spell then ends.

**Classes**: [Cleric (Nature Domain)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/cleric-nature-domain.md), [Cleric](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/cleric.md), [Druid (Circle of the Land)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/druid-circle-of-the-land.md), [Druid](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/druid.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 272. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
