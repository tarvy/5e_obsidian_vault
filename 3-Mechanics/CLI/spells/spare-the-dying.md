# Spare the Dying
*cantrip, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a living creature that has 0 hit points. The creature becomes stable. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

**Classes**: [Cleric (Death Domain)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/cleric-death-domain.md), [Cleric](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/cleric.md), [Warlock (The Undying)](/3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/warlock-the-undying-scag.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 277. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*
